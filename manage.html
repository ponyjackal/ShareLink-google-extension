How it works

Outwardly

This is a simple link sharing extension. It allows you to easily send a link to a friend and have it show up directly in their browser. The basic workflow is:
  1. You and your friend both install this extension.
  2. You enter your name and email.
  3. You enter your friend's name and email.
  4. Your friend enters the same information.
  5. When you are on a page you want to send to the friend, click the extension icon in the browser toolbar and click the Save icon. The link is immediately sent to your friend's extension.
  6. When your friend sends you a link, it will appear in your list.

Internally

  1. When you save link for your friend, it is saved to a internal database within your browser.
  2. This data is then synced with a Couchdb server in the cloud (or you can use your own).
  3. When your friend opens their browser, their internal database syncs with the same Couchdb server.
  4. It pulls down all the links that have their email address attached to it.
  5. When you open your browser, your local database is synced with all links that have your email address attached to it.

This is why both users need to use the same email addresses: It is common (foreign) key between the link sharers.
